Crispy and Golden

Hashbrowns

Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes, peeled
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Grate the potatoes and rinse under cold water. Squeeze out as much moisture as possible using a towel.
  2. Heat butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the grated potatoes, spreading them out evenly. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook until the bottom is golden brown and crispy, about 5 minutes. Flip and cook the other side.
  4. Serve hot, garnished with fresh herbs if desired.
