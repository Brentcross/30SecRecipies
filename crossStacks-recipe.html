German Pancake Stacks (Cross Stacks)

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Powdered sugar, for serving
  • Fresh berries, for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Place the butter in a large ovenproof skillet and melt in the oven.
  2. In a blender, combine the eggs, milk, flour, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour the batter into the preheated skillet, over the melted butter. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the pancake is puffed and golden.
  4. Remove from oven, cut into stacks, and serve immediately topped with powdered sugar and fresh berries.
